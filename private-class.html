Why Choose Private Classes?

100% About You

Group courses teach everyone the same thing. Private classes teach you exactly what you need. Whether you want to prepare for a trip, speak with a Thai partner's family, advance in your career, or simply progress faster than any course allows — private sessions with Mind are your shortcut.

Every session is planned around your current level, your specific weak spots, and your personal goals. No time wasted on things you already know. No waiting for others to catch up.
