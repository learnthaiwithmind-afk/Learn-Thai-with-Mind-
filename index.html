Meet Your Teacher

Hi, I'm Mind
— your guide to Thai

I've spent over a decade sharing the beauty of the Thai language with students from all over the world — and I genuinely love every moment of it. My lessons are built on the belief that language learning should be joyful, practical, and deeply rewarding.

"Thai is not just a language — it's a window into one of the world's richest cultures. I want every student to walk away not just speaking Thai, but loving it."

I teach all four skills — speaking, listening, reading, and writing — across every level from absolute beginner to advanced. My approach blends structured knowledge with the kind of practical conversational Thai that you'll actually use in real life. Expect laughter, real insight, and measurable progress.
