Meet Your Teacher

Hi, I'm Mind — Wasina Rukvichai

I'm a Thai language teacher based in Chiang Mai, Thailand, with over a decade of experience teaching students from all around the world. Everyone calls me Mind — and that's where the name comes from. My real passion is helping you understand Thai by heart: how the language truly works, how Thai people actually speak, and the culture behind every word.

"I don't teach textbook Thai — I teach the Thai that Thais actually use every day. The language, the slang, the culture. That's what makes it real."

I didn't graduate from a language field, but my passion for language has always driven me. I love finding new ways to explain things clearly so my students have a genuine "aha!" moment. Students always tell me that studying with me is both enjoyable and deeply knowledgeable — because I teach both the language and the culture together.

Master Thai on Your Terms —
Language, Culture & Confidence

Learn Thai at your own pace, in your own style. From your very first word to fluent conversation — with the cultural understanding to truly connect with Thailand and its people.

Real Thai, Not Textbook Thai

Mind teaches the Thai people actually speak — natural expressions, everyday slang, and the cultural nuance that makes language feel alive. You'll understand real conversations, not just scripted dialogues.

Your Pace, Your Style

Every learner is different — and that's celebrated here. Study on weekday mornings, weekend evenings, or a quiet Sunday. Rewatch, go slow, skip ahead. Your Thai journey runs on your schedule, not anyone else's.

All Levels, All Skills

Never studied Thai before? Already conversational? There's a course for exactly where you are. Speaking, listening, reading, and writing — from your first สวัสดี to reading Thai signs with confidence.

Culture, Etiquette & Respect

Language without culture is only half the picture. Learn when to wai, how to show respect, what kreng jai really means — and why it matters. Thais will feel your genuine understanding, and that opens every door.

Language + Culture Together

Sanook. Kreng jai. Mai pen rai. Every Thai phrase carries a world of meaning. Mind weaves cultural insight through every lesson — so you don't just speak Thai, you truly understand Thailand.

Study Anywhere, Anytime

HD video lessons, PDF workbooks, and lifetime access — on any device. Study from your sofa, a café in Chiang Mai, or during your commute. Your Thai classroom travels with you, wherever you go.
